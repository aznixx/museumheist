GAME DESIGN SUMMARY
===================
SETTING: Egyptian museum with multiple interconnected rooms

GAMEPLAY LOOP (per run, 30-45 min):
1. Briefing (2 min) - Show artifact location, guards, map
2. Planning (3-5 min) - Team talks strategy
3. Infiltration (10-15 min) - Sneak through museum
4. Theft (1 min) - Grab artifact, alarms trigger
5. Extraction (5-10 min) - Race to exit
6. Result (1 min) - Success or restart

DIFFICULTY LEVELS:
- Easy: 5 min timer, fewer guards
- Normal: 4 min timer, standard guards
- Hard: 3 min timer, more guards

REPLAYABILITY:
- Procedural guard positions (different each run)
- Multiple escape routes
- Secondary loot for bonus points
- Leaderboard scores

PLATFORMS:
- PC (Steam) first
- 1-4 player co-op
- Proximity voice chat
